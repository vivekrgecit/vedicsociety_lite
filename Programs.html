The programs shall be conducted in English and/or Hindi Language.

  1. BRAHMAVIDYA (VEDA/VEDANTA) – The Programs on Brahmavidya (Veda/Vedanta) for Brahmajnan “Know the Parama Brahma/Supreme God” shall be conducted weekly on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.
  2. SHRIMADBHAGWAT GEETA – The Programs on “Shrimadbhagwat Geeta” shall be conducted on every Sunday.
  3. SAMATA (NEUTRALITY) – The Universal Vedic Society will organize two weeks Program on time to time to learn and assimilate SAMATA/NEUTRALITY. This program will help people to be mentally strong and will develop strong will power and determination to achieve the objectives.
  4. SPECIAL PROGRAMS FOR CHILDREN (9 YEAR TO 16 YEAR) – The Universal Vedic Society will organize special programs on time to time for children aged between 9 to 16 years to make them learn and assimilate the Vedic Values/Sanskar to make them strong, civil and responsible persons towards family and society.
