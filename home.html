Mahadev Shiva is the Supreme God and is the creator of whole universe. Shivalinga, represents Mahadev Shiva, is the Shaktipunj and is the source of all energy and life and is operating the universe.

With the inspiration and blessings of Mahadev Shiva and with the objectives of peaceful and prosperous universe the Vedic Society came into birth. The Vedic Society is going to establish the BRAHMAVIDYAPEETH in the holy city Rishikesh, Himalayas, India.

The Vedic Society believes that the knowledge and principles of the Vedas, Puranas, Upnishads, Shrimadbhagwat Geeta and Shastras is for the people of whole world. The people of whole world can make their lives peaceful and prosperous with the knowledge and principles of the Vedas, Puranas, Upnishads, Shrimadbhagwat Geeta and Shastras.

The Vedic Society shall conduct programs and events in respect of the Vedas, Puranas, Upnishads, Shrimadbhagwat Geeta and Shastras so that the people of whole world can learn and assimilate the knowledge and principles of the Vedas, Puranas, Upnishads, Shrimadbhagwat Geeta and Shastras.
